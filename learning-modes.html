Three Ways to Learn

We meet you where you are. Choose the learning format that fits your lifestyle and ministry calling.

🏫

Onsite Learning

Traditional classroom-based education at our campus. Experience the full college community life with chapel services, mentoring, and face-to-face instruction.

  • Mon–Fri structured schedule
  • Direct faculty mentoring
  • Library & campus access
  • Chapel & community life
  • Residential options available
Apply for Onsite
💻

Online Learning

Fully digital courses delivered through our Moodle LMS. Study at your own pace with live sessions, video lectures, and digital resources accessible 24/7.

  • Self-paced & guided tracks
  • Live virtual class sessions
  • Moodle LMS platform
  • Mobile-friendly access
  • Email & chat support
Access Online Portal
📚

Modular Learning

Printed and digital study modules for those in remote areas or with limited internet access. Study on your own schedule with periodic in-person assessments.

  • Printed module packets
  • Offline-capable study
  • Periodic assessment days
  • Phone/email tutoring
  • Ideal for remote learners
